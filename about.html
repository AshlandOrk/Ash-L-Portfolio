About me

I'm a jack-of-all-trades in a world of specialists, which I supppose makes me my own kind of specialist as a result.

Background

I grew up with a deep love of nature and a fascination with games and systems. Most of my childhood was divided between playing games with my friends, and hiking through the local woods to take photos and turn over rocks.

Starting in 2013 I attended Sheridan College for 4 years as part of the first class of its Honors Bachelor of Arts in Game Design program, which I graduated from in 2017. The diverse courses within the program helped me to develop a range of skills development skills from project management, art & design, programming, testing, and marketing.

After Graduating from the program I moved to Hamilton in order to start fresh and spent the next few years moving between jobs as I built up my skills and searched for a sense of purpose. I spent time as a grocery clerk, a youth worker, an art seller, and a volunteer farm hand.

Interests

My primary interests all centre around systems and connections; I love to study ecology, techology, and community. I love studying novel experiences, learning new techniques, and discovering new solutions.
